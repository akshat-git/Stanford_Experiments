GRPO - Group Relative Policy Optimization
- Starts by forcing model to think, 
    - output its reasoning within <think>...</think> tags
    - output answers within <answer>...</answer> tags
    - make M separate outputs with thought processes, penalize skipping/misuse of tags, optimize step
- Simple accuracy checker on answer tags
    - N of M outputs accurate, find group average accuracy score
    - penalize relative inaccuracy and reinforce relative accuracy
- Penalizes less complex or misguided thought processes along with inaccuracy
- Reinforces complex thought with accurate solutions
- Result is model with 'thought' processes, eliminates less viable logic patterns for problems
